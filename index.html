Marketing Department

Led by Junaid Khan, the Marketing Department focuses on promoting Japan Resource Group's services, building strong client relationships, and creating brand awareness. The team ensures effective communication, manages campaigns, and highlights the company's strengths in the logistics industry.

Transport Department

Headed by Shiraz, the Transport Department manages land and ocean transportation. It ensures smooth cargo movement, timely deliveries, and compliance with safety and regulatory standards while coordinating with other departments for efficiency.

Booking Department

Also overseen by Shiraz, the Booking Department handles cargo reservations and shipment scheduling. The team ensures accurate documentation, timely bookings, and seamless coordination with carriers and clients.

Operation Department

Managed by Junaid Khan, the Operation Department is responsible for day-to-day logistics execution. From handling shipments to coordinating with transport and yard services, this department ensures that all operations run smoothly and efficiently.
