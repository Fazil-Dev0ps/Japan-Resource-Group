EXPERT VANNING & INSPECTION

Pre-Shipment Quality & Safety checks

At Japan Resource Group, we provide professional loading (vanning) and pre-shipment inspections tailored for vehicles and specialized cargo. Our rigorous inspection process ensures every vehicle is documented, safe, and meets strict quality standards before export. Coupled with our efficient vanning techniques, we maximize container space without compromising cargo safety.

Optimized Vanning Layouts

We utilize precision loading plans to accommodate varying unit sizes safely:
Standard 1-2 Units for heavy/oversized vehicles
Stacked 3-4 Units for sedans and compact cars
Maximized 5 Units for light motorcars and hatchbacks
